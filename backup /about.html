Features


King:
This feature identifies the top gainer of the day, awarding it the title of “King,” and similarly highlights the top loser as “Pawn”. This simple visual cue helps users immediately see which coins are dominating the market or underperforming.


Grow:
Provides a live comparison of coin growth across multiple cryptocurrencies. Users can quickly understand which coins are gaining momentum and which are stagnating, offering a clear view of market dynamics.


HG (Hidden Gems):
Highlights fast-growing but lesser-known coins. These hidden gems may not yet be popular but have significant growth potential, helping users discover opportunities before they become mainstream.


Pred:
Offers probability analysis for potential price returns or reversals. By studying past patterns and current momentum, users can see which coins may be more likely to reverse trends, allowing for smarter observation and decision-making.


Airdrop:
Alerts users about upcoming or potential airdrops, ensuring they don’t miss opportunities to acquire new tokens from emerging projects.


Power:
Displays the live strength of coins in the market, giving a quick and visual metric for which coins are currently performing strongly and attempting to gain dominance.
