📊 Probability of Market Reversal

This section provides a **crypto market analysis** that predicts the probability of a coin’s next movement. It helps users understand whether a particular cryptocurrency is likely to experience a gain or a loss in the near future.

The analysis is visually represented using **colored circles**:

  • 🔴 Red Circle – indicates potential loss
  • 🟢 Green Circle – indicates potential gain
The percentage inside the circle shows the **probability of a reversal**, helping traders anticipate possible market changes.

Users can **test historical data** over 1–10 days by adjusting the day selector. This feature allows for backtesting and understanding how the analysis would have performed in previous market conditions.

The page uses Crypotoken’s algorithm to analyze **price patterns, trends, and volatility** across multiple cryptocurrencies. By calculating the likelihood of reversals, traders can make more informed decisions about when to enter or exit positions.

In practical terms, the Probability of Market Reversal helps users:

  • Identify coins likely to switch from loss to gain or gain to loss
  • Plan trades with higher confidence
  • Reduce risk by understanding potential market swings

Overall, this page provides a **predictive overview of market movements**, showing probabilities rather than certainties. It is a tool for smarter trading and better risk management, allowing users to anticipate potential reversals before they happen.
